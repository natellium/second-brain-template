3. **Identify action items** — for each remaining email, extract any tasks or follow-ups. Pay special attention to emails from Workday (HR actions, approvals, etc.). Add new to-dos to `task-board.md` under the appropriate section. Do not duplicate items already there. Do not group the items.

   - Meeting invites captured
   - Newsletter highlights
   - Any tasks added to the task board
   Do not duplicate content that's already in the note. Append new sections or bullets only.

7. **Mark emails as read** — mark all meeting invites and sales/marketing emails as read. Leave other emails unread unless they've been fully actioned.
