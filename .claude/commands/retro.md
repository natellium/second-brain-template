Personal quarterly retrospective. What I focused on, what moved, what I'd do differently.

Distinct from `/quarterly-review` (which is team-facing and goal-oriented). This is inward-looking.

---

## Step 1 — Determine the quarter

Ask: "Which quarter? (default: the quarter just ended)"

Determine the date range (e.g. Q1 2026 = Jan 1 – Mar 31).

---

## Step 2 — Read the quarter's content

Read all of the following from the quarter's date range:
- Daily notes in `daily-notes/`
- All files in `learnings/`
- All monthly and weekly reviews in `reviews/`
- Both team strategy files and quarter files

---

## Step 3 — Analyse

Reflect across the full quarter:

- **What I focused on** — where did my time and attention actually go?
- **What moved** — things that made real progress
- **What got stuck** — repeated deferrals, blockers, things that never resolved
- **What I learned** — from `learnings/` and from patterns in the daily notes
- **Effort vs impact** — any mismatch between where I spent time and what mattered?

---

## Step 4 — Identify carry-forwards

Identify:
- **3 things to carry forward** — priorities, habits, or approaches worth continuing
- **3 things to drop or change** — things that didn't serve me well this quarter

---

Write to `reviews/retro/YYYY-QN-retro.md`:

```
---

## What Moved

```

---

## Step 6 — Update memory

Read `.claude/memory.md`. Update with key strategic context from the retro — decisions, commitments, or shifts in direction worth carrying into next quarter.

Write the updated memory file.
